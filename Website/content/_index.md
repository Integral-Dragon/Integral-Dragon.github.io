## Who We Are

Integral Dragon is a software engineering transformation consultancy based in Minneapolis, Minnesota, specializing in building operational excellence for enterprise technology organizations. We work with US-based companies across industries to strengthen their engineering foundations through strategic improvements to People, Process, and Technology—then accelerate their business value through intelligent AI integration.

Founded in 2025 by Dragon Sky, a former Platform Engineer at UnitedHealth Group with deep expertise in distributed systems, platforming, cloud architecture, and building high-performing engineering cultures, Integral Dragon brings decades of collective software engineering and consulting experience to every engagement.

## What Makes Us Different

**While others rush to apply AI as a quick fix, we build for lasting transformation.**

The market is flooded with AI-first solutions promising instant acceleration. But here's what we've learned: AI amplifies what you already have. If your engineering operations are inefficient, your processes unclear, or your teams siloed—AI will only accelerate those problems.

We take a different approach. We start by galvanizing your engineering foundation through rigorous assessment and strategic enhancement of your people, processes, and technology. Only then do we introduce AI—strategically, measurably, and where it delivers genuine business value.

**We don't use AI as a bandaid. We build businesses the right way.**

Our methodology combines proven software engineering excellence with modern AI capabilities, ensuring every transformation delivers measurable ROI and positions your organization for sustained competitive advantage.

## Measuring Success: The Integral Metrics Framework

We track transformation through three interconnected metric categories:

### Business Metrics
- Value booked with the CFO
- Cost of implementation
- Value per engineer ratio
- Per-transaction cost trends

### Engineering Metrics (DORA)
- Deployment frequency
- Lead time for changes
- Change failure rate
- Mean time to restore
- Bad engineering days

### AI Acceleration Metrics
- AI-assisted productivity realized
- Code suggestions vs. acceptance rates
- Percentage of AI-generated code
